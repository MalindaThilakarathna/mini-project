# 🏫 Campus Lost & Found - Quick Start Guide

## ⚡ Quick Launch (2 Steps)

### Step 1: Open the Website
- Find the folder `campus_lost_found`
- Double-click on **`index.html`**
- Your browser will open automatically

### Step 2: Start Using!
- Explore the homepage
- Report lost or found items
- Search for items
- Contact other students

---

## 📖 Main Features at a Glance

### 🏠 **Homepage** (index.html)
- Overview of the system
- Show statistics (Lost items count, Found items count, Returned items)
- See 6 most recent items reported
- Links to other pages

### 📝 **Report Lost Item** (report-lost.html)
1. Enter item name
2. Describe the item in detail
3. Say where you lost it
4. Pick the date you lost it
5. Upload a photo (optional)
6. Give your email and phone number
7. Click "Submit Report"
✅ Your item appears instantly on the search page!

### 🔍 **Report Found Item** (report-found.html)
1. Enter item name
2. Describe what you found
3. Say where you found it
4. Pick the date you found it
5. Upload a photo
6. Give your email and phone
7. Say where you're keeping it
8. Click "Submit Found Item"
✅ Help return items to owners!

### 🔎 **Search Items** (search.html)
- **Search box**: Type item name or description
- **Status filter**: Choose Lost, Found, or All Items
- **Location filter**: Filter by location
- **Click any item card** to see full details and contact info
- **Clear Filters** button to reset

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is my personal information safe?**
A: Your contact info is only shown to people looking at your item. It's not shared anywhere else.

**Q: How long is data saved?**
A: Until you clear your browser's cache/history. Otherwise, permanently!

**Q: Can I edit my posts?**
A: Current version doesn't support editing. Delete and re-post if needed.

**Q: What if someone is being mean?**
A: Contact Campus Security or email: support@campuslostfound.lk

**Q: Can I delete an item?**
A: Yes, but contact admin. The app doesn't have delete buttons in current version.

**Q: Works on my phone?**
A: Yes! Open index.html in any phone browser. Fully responsive!

## 🚨 Troubleshooting

### Problem: Website not opening
- **Solution**: Make sure you double-clicked `index.html`
- **Alternative**: Drag `index.html` into your browser window

### Problem: Forms not submitting
- **Solution**: Make sure all required fields are filled (marked with *)
- **Check**: Email format should be valid (example@university.edu)

### Problem: Items not saved
- **Solution**: Check if localStorage is enabled in browser settings
- **Note**: Some private browsing modes disable localStorage

### Problem: Images not showing
- **Solution**: Use common formats (JPG, PNG, GIF)
- **Note**: Very large files might not upload

### Problem: Items disappeared
- **Cause**: Browser cache was cleared
- **Solution**: Re-submit items or restore from browser history
